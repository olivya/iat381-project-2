Brad Smith

Brad Smith and John Lee are both professional photographers with over 20 years of experience each. Brad has experience working in the many fields of photography that range from weddings, studio shoots, fashion, and more. Brad has worked for some of the top magazines doing photo shoots with celebrities. John has spent many years in photographing nature, sports, and weddings. However his major role in the company is the lead post productionist. He edits the majority of the photos, and Brad takes majority of the photos. Both are exceptionally talented in both photography and post-production, and together they form an elite partnership that captures world-class art through the lens of a camera and can make it better with technical aids.
